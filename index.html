About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Finance Department at the Purdue University Daniels School of Business. My research focuses on corporate finance theory, asset pricing, financial econometrics, and behavioral finance.


Prior to joining Purdue, I was a Visiting Assistant Professor at the NYU Department of Economics (AY 2021–2022). My Erdős number is 4.
